Create a new chat

Welcome to bivouac. This service lets you create non-public group chat rooms with easy filesharing - literally in just one click.

Simply hit the Create and enter chat button below, and your personal chat is instantly available. Send the URL of the chat to everyone you want to invite - because the URL is non-public, noone else will be able to find your chat.
